Word Game



FAQ



What is this game?


This is a clone of the game 'Wordle' by the New York Times.

How do I play this game?


You have 6 attempts to guess a word that is five letters long. If you don't get it, don't worry as the answer is shown at the end.

Do I have to wait until tomorrow for another word, like Wordle?


No. This is an unlimited version meaning if you want to try another word, just refresh your browser.

The game accepted a offensive word! What do I do?


We apologize and this may be because of a error in the filter. Please refresh, if the word still is being accepted, let us know.

What font do you use?


We use DSans with Montserrat and Arial as a back-up font if DSans doesn't load or is not supported.
